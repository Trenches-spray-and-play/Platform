## ğŸš« Forbidden Phrases (All Voices)

These phrases trigger compliance review and brand damage. **Never use**:

| Forbidden | Why | Alternative |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| "Guaranteed returns" | Securities violation | "Targeted settlement" or "Designed for 1.5x" |
| "Risk-free" | Misleading | "Non-custodial" or "Transparent mechanics" |
| "Get rich quick" | Scam association | "Capital-efficient distribution" |
| "100% certain" | Promise of outcome | "Mathematically structured" |
| "Free money" | Devalues the work | "Earned rewards" or "Marketing compensation" |
| "Moon" / "Lambo" / "100x" | Meme coin association | "Strategic positioning" |
| "Guaranteed profit" | Securities violation | "Fixed settlement target" |

## âœ… Voice-Specific Guidelines

### INSTITUTIONAL ELITE

**Characteristics**:
- No contractions (use "do not" not "don't")
- No emojis
- Technical terminology acceptable
- Third-person perspective
- Focus on mechanics, not outcomes

**Do**:
- "Trenches facilitates non-custodial token distribution through a queue-based coordination layer."
- "Capital deployment triggers token acquisition via audited smart contracts."

**Don't**:
- âŒ "You'll make 1.5x your money!"
- âŒ "Get in now before it's too late!"
- âŒ "This is gonna be huge ğŸš€"

### ACCESSIBLE INSTITUTIONAL

**Characteristics**:
- Contractions OK
- Max 2 emojis per post
- Explain technical terms on first use
- Second-person perspective ("you")
- Focus on user benefit + mechanics

**Do**:
- "Your deposit buys the project's token. Then you help them grow on social media."
- "Turn $1,000 into $1,500 in 24 hours. Here's how it works..."
- "Ready to start? Join the waitlist. ğŸ›¡ï¸"
